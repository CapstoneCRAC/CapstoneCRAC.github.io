This project focuses on helping learners hear and practise pronunciation contrasts that are often difficult in Mandarin learning. The app breaks the practice into short activities so that learners can repeat one sound pair at a time before moving on.

The first activity asks learners to listen to a target sound and choose between similar finals or initials. After each attempt, the app gives immediate feedback so learners can notice the difference and try again without waiting for classroom correction.

The gallery above shows a simple learning path: start from the home screen, enter a practice task, and review feedback after the activity. In a full project record, this section can be expanded into a short blog-style story about the design idea, target users, and learning outcome.
