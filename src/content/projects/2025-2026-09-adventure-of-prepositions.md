The Adventure of Prepositions is designed as a keyboard-controlled 2D learning game for beginners, primary-school pupils and learners who need extra support with English. Instead of meeting prepositions only on a worksheet, players are asked to act on on-screen instructions as they move a character through an environment.

The project concept uses themed locations — including a city, forest, jungle, desert and snow setting — to create obstacles and actions such as “jump over the river” or “walk through the tunnel.” Points and immediate feedback are intended to encourage repeated attempts, while the action itself connects the word with a visible movement and location. The report describes this as a proposed educational game, so the page does not state that the planned outcomes were measured or delivered.

## Learning by moving through space

- Keyboard-controlled movement in a web-based 2D game
- Themed city, forest, jungle, desert and snow environments
- Action prompts that use prepositions in context
- Point tracking for successful tasks
- Immediate corrective feedback and another chance to try
