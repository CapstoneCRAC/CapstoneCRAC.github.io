The proposal treats prepositions as verbs in motion, not blanks to fill in. A learner is asked to jump over a river or walk through a tunnel, using the keyboard to make the word happen on screen. The report keeps that idea at the centre of its proposed 2D game.

City, forest, jungle, desert and snow settings create different routes and obstacles, while points and immediate feedback invite another attempt. The project uses movement to make the meaning of each preposition immediately visible.

## Learning by moving through space

- Keyboard-controlled movement in a web-based 2D game
- Themed city, forest, jungle, desert and snow environments
- Action prompts that use prepositions in context
- Point tracking for successful tasks
- Immediate corrective feedback and another chance to try
