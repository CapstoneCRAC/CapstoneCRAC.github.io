BlendItOut supports children aged 4–6 as they learn to hear, segment and blend consonant-vowel-consonant words. Its central Segment & Blend activity uses straight and curved lines to show how individual sounds join together, while replay controls let learners return to a sound when they need another listen. The app treats repeated practice as part of a colourful, low-pressure learning journey.

The experience then moves from guided blending to a growing set of CVC challenges. Learners can explore word families, spin for a missing consonant and play short activities such as CVC Connect, Whack The Alien, Grab & Read and CVC Space Adventure. Across these activities, visual prompts, audio interaction and simple rewards keep attention on the sound patterns that make early reading possible.

## From guided blending to mini-games

- Segment & Blend with visual lines that show individual sounds joining into a word
- Replayable sound dots for revisiting each phoneme
- Word-family practice and a spin-the-wheel missing-consonant activity
- Four CVC Challenge mini-games: CVC Connect, Whack The Alien, Grab & Read and CVC Space Adventure
- Visual, audio and reward-based feedback for repeated practice

## Current build

The final report documents an integrated browser-based app with the Segment & Blend activity and four CVC Challenge games. Testing and refinement were reported, with mobile and iPad optimisation identified as future work.
