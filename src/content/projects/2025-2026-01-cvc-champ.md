The proposal’s core gesture was a line: straight or curved, it lets a young learner see individual phonemes travel together into a CVC word. The final report shows that idea becoming BlendItOut’s Segment & Blend activity, with replayable sound dots so a child can listen again before trying the word.

From that gentle starting point, the reported build opens into word families, a missing-consonant spinner and four short CVC challenges — CVC Connect, Whack The Alien, Grab & Read and CVC Space Adventure. The colourful rewards are not the main lesson; they create a reason to stay with the sound pattern long enough for blending to become familiar.

## From guided blending to mini-games

- Segment & Blend with visual lines that show individual sounds joining into a word
- Replayable sound dots for revisiting each phoneme
- Word-family practice and a spin-the-wheel missing-consonant activity
- Four CVC Challenge mini-games: CVC Connect, Whack The Alien, Grab & Read and CVC Space Adventure
- Visual, audio and reward-based feedback for repeated practice

## Current build

The final report documents an integrated browser-based app with the Segment & Blend activity and four CVC Challenge games.
